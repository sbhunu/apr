# APR System Training Materials

## Training Program Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive training program for the Automated Property Registration (APR) system.

## Training Objectives

By the end of training, participants will be able to:

1. Navigate the APR system confidently
2. Complete their role-specific workflows
3. Understand system features and capabilities
4. Troubleshoot common issues
5. Access help and support resources

## Assessment and Certification

### Assessment Components

1. **Practical Assessment** (60%)
   - Complete assigned workflows
   - Demonstrate competency
   - Handle error scenarios

2. **Knowledge Test** (30%)
   - Multiple choice questions
   - True/false questions
   - Scenario-based questions

3. **Feedback and Participation** (10%)
   - Active participation
   - Question quality
   - Peer collaboration

### Certification Requirements

- Minimum 80% score on practical assessment
- Minimum 75% score on knowledge test
- Attendance at all training sessions
- Completion of all practice exercises

### Certification Levels

- **Basic User**: Completed foundation training
- **Advanced User**: Completed role-specific training + assessment
- **Power User**: Completed all modules + advanced assessment
- **Trainer**: Power User + train-the-trainer program

## Training Delivery Methods

### In-Person Training

- **Location**: Training center or office
- **Duration**: 2 weeks (10 days)
- **Group Size**: 10-15 participants
- **Equipment**: Provided computers
- **Materials**: Printed manuals and guides

### Online Training

- **Platform**: Video conferencing + LMS
- **Duration**: 2 weeks (flexible schedule)
- **Group Size**: 10-15 participants
- **Equipment**: Participant's own computer
- **Materials**: Digital manuals and guides

### Self-Paced Learning

- **Platform**: Online learning management system
- **Duration**: 4 weeks (flexible)
- **Materials**: Video tutorials, guides, exercises
- **Support**: Email and forum support

## Best Practices

### For Trainers

1. Prepare thoroughly before each session
2. Use real-world examples
3. Encourage questions and participation
4. Provide hands-on practice time
5. Follow up with participants

### For Trainees

1. Attend all sessions
2. Complete practice exercises
3. Ask questions when unclear
4. Take notes during training
5. Practice regularly after training

### For Organizations

1. Schedule training before go-live
2. Provide dedicated training time
3. Ensure all users complete training
4. Support ongoing learning
5. Collect and act on feedback
